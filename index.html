About Me

During my eight years in pet cremation, I successfully managed a team dedicated to providing compassionate and respectful services to our clients. My job entailed overseeing daily operations, ensuring compliance with industry standards, and implementing process improvements to enhance efficiency and service quality. My role demanded a high level of empathy, excellent communication skills, and strong attention to detail, all of which I applied to support grieving pet owners and maintain a supportive work environment for my.


Currently, I am embarking on an exciting new journey in the tech world, undertaking a full stack deep dive coding boot camp. This rigorous program is equipping me with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in software development. I am becoming proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Next.js, Typescript, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, Insomnia, Tailwind CSS, and various frameworks. I am eager to leverage my problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, and leadership experience in this new field.


Family is very important to me, and I am a proud parent of four wonderful kids. Balancing my professional and personal life has taught me the importance of time management and being present in everything I do.
