Purpose

One of the things that I would like to accomplish with my PWP is gaining full time employment.
I want my page to not only show that I have developed the skills needed to be a proficient developer, but to set me apart from competitors.
My PWP will highlight the qualities that make me a great team member, as well as a hard worker with a great work ethic.
When a potential employer views my page, they will be able to see that I am dedicated, I am hardworking, and thanks to my PWP I am qualified.

Audience

The audience that will be looking at my PWP will be potential employers hiring for full-time employment or freelance opportunities.
Project/hiring managers that may not be where I am from, trying to extract enough information to make an informed decision in a limited time frame.
Another audience would be a small business that want to extend their presence on the internet via a web page.

Goal

I want to create a place where my work can be displayed for potential employers to see. I want to ba able to use my PWP as a way to land potential
part-time or full-time Dev work. The type of people that will be looking at this site are going to be senior developers, hiring managers, and small.
business owners. I would like for this site to show any project manager, business owner that I can be a strong addition to any team.

Persona

Jacob Anderson is senior developer with a major non-tech-based company. This company has an extremely complex asset tracking system that spans over
mobile apps and webpages. His corporate level bosses are looking for a way to streamline communication within the company that has locations all over the country.
Jacob has been put in charge of this latest project. he was given full oversight on hiring the team that will need to build to complete that task at hand.


Jacob is running a 2023 imac desktop all in one computer with the latest MacOS14.
The 2024 macbook air also with latest macOS14, Iphone 14 pro with iOS17.5.1.


Not only is Jacob frustrated by the amount of responsibility that has been put on his shoulders, but he really doesn't have time to read through
a massive stack of resumes. Also, there have not been a good amount of options coming from where Jacob lives; so he is going to have to expand his search
for prospective employees to job search engines like linked in ect. Jacob hopes to minimize the time and maximize the results of his search by requesting his candidates
send him previews of their work, or some kind of portfolio. This will allow Jacob to not only learn about potential hires, but to see the quality of the work they have done in the past
