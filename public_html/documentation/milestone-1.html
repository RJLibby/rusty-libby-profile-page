Purpose

One of the main things that I would like my PWP to accomplish is to assist me in getting a job that is not just a job, but a career.
I want my page to not only show that I have developed the skills needed to be a proficient developer, but to set me apart from competitors.
My PWP will highlight the qualities that make me a great team member, as well as a hard worker with a great work ethic.
When a potential job prospect views my page, they are gonna know that I am dedicated, hardworking, qualified and that hiring me would be the
best decision they could make for the job.

Audience

The audience that will be looking at my PWP will be potential employers. Be it a company, or somebody looking for someone to do freelance work.
These are the two types of audiences that I will be designing my page for. This could be a senior developer with a big company, or a small business owner.
looking to make their internet presence greater.

Goal

I want to create a place where my work can be displayed for potential employers to see. I want to ba able to use my PWP as a way to land potential
part-time and full-time work. The type of people that will be looking at this site are going to be needing to find someone to add to their team.
I would like for this site to show any project manager, business owner, or hiring manager that I can be a strong addition to any team.
